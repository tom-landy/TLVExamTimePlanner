One Week View

Weekly exam task calendar

Choose one exam, set which days the candidate is in, then place that exam's tasks into the week's timetable.

How it works

Pick `ESP` or `OSP`, then use the calendar itself to mark whether the candidate is in and what times apply.

Setup

Student and exam

Use the calendar tick box to show whether the candidate is in that day. Fixed breaks are always removed: 11:10 to 11:25, 13:35 to 14:15, and 15:20 to 15:25.

Tasks

Choose one task, then place it into the calendar.

Time edits update the calendar after a valid time is entered. Tasks still split automatically around the fixed breaks.
